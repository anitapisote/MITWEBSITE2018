The ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING programme was started in 1984 with an intake capacity of 30 students. Now the department is known as ELECTRONICS AND TELECOMMUNICATION ENGINEERING (ETC) department. Being affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere, the department offers full time B. Tech (ETC) course (Intake 120) and M. Tech in Embedded Systems course (Intake-24).

The aim of the department is to provide technical education of the highest standard in the field of electronics and telecommunication. The Electronics & Telecommunication engineering branch includes the study of basic electronics, advanced electronics related to the ever-changing telecommunication field.

The strength of the department lies in highly qualified and experienced staff members who are serving the college from last 10 to 23 years.

The department has excellent state of the art laboratories. The department also has Electronics workshop and large number of software tools for students to use.

The industry institution interaction is also maintained by arranging guest lectures, seminars, technical workshops and industrial visits. The students interact with industrial experts during industrial visit and seminars to bridge the gap between theory and practice.

The students are updated with state of the art technology, allowing our graduates to work at the forefront of all major areas of Electronics and Telecommunication field. Workshops on preparation for aptitude tests and interviews for placement in MNCs are conducted for final year students.

Apart from academic excellence, the department also encourages students to take part in various co-curricular and extra co-curricular activities. MIT provides a platform for students to develop organizational skills and to upgrade their technical competence.

The department conducts a national level technical symposium 'E-Nexplora'' annually for the benefit of the students. Also our students participate in a National level Robotic contest ROBOCON every year. Our students have received prizes at different state and national level competitions.

Many undergraduate students undergo training in industries and government sectors like BSNL. The students passed out from ETC department have been successfully placed in various private and public sector organizations at recognised positions. The alumni of our department are serving society all over the globe. The department also encourages the students to be entrepreneurs.
