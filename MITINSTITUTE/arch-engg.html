    Marathwada Institute of Technology, Aurangabad (Engineering College)


    An Engineering College since 1984, approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, and Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Mumbai. This Institution is affiliated to the Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University, Aurangabad since its inception. However the courses under this Institute are affiliated to Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, a state central Technological university located at Lonere in Raigad district, Maharashtra, India with effect from the academic year 2017 – 2018 starting from first year of each course.
    For more information, please visit: MIT Engineering
