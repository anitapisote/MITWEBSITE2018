Kalavihangam-2018

MIT students enjoyed “Kalavinhangam-2018” Annual gathering In MIT Aurangabad, Three days “Kalavinhangam-2018” Annual gathering was celebrated on 26, 27 and 28 Feb 2018. On first day the inauguration was done in presence on Mr. N Shreeram, President and director, Endress+Hauser Flowtec (India) Pvt. Ltd. Aurangbad, MIT director general Prof. Munish Sharma, Principal Dr. Santosh Bhosale, Principal Dr. Nilesh Patil, Principal Prof. Sunil Deshmukh, Principal Dr. P S Kadam, Principal prof. Helen Rani, all professor and students. At starting MIT students group performed the Dhol performance. On the same day Indie Routes, Abhas Joshi and Shreyas Joshi, The voice of India, A famous Band from Mumbai entertained studentes by playing different Marathi, Hindi, Punjabi, Sufi and Pop songs. In these three MIT students artist performed their cultural activities of solo songs, solo dance, powada, duet and other events in this cultural event. Dr. Ajay Dahake and their group performed the skit on “Doctors for beggers”. The MIT students from Bhutan country performed event on culture of Bhutan. All faculties and students representatives took lot of efforts for successly completing this event.
