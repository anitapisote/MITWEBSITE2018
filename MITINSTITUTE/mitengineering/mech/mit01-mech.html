    About Us

    The Mechanical Engineering Department started right at the inception of Marathwada Institute of Technology Aurangabad. Since its inception, the Mechanical Engineering Department is well known for the quality of teaching and the supporting environment provided to the students.

    To produce all round mechanical engineers with human values, innovator, solution provider and researcher so as to serve society as a whole.

    Mechanical engineering department is committed to excel through appropriate and sustainable learning environment, industry partnership, innovative teaching and learning process, state of the art laboratories, talented teachers and continuous improvement.

    • To impart fundamental and applied education in the field of Mechanical Engineering that will lead to multidimensional development of students.

    • To develop professional abilities among students for problem solving in Mechanical and allied domains of engineering.

    • To create awareness of social implications, research aptitude, self-development and entrepreneurship skills.

    • Engineering knowledge: Mechanical Engineering graduates will acquire and be able to demonstrate fundamental knowledge of Mathematics, Science, Thermal, Design, Materials and Manufacturing

    • Problem analysis: Graduates will be able to identify, define, formulate and solve Thermal, Design, Materials and Manufacturing Engineering problems.

    • Design/development of solutions: Graduates will demonstrate an ability to design and conduct scientific experiments, analyze, interpret the data and report the results.

    • Conduct investigations of complex problems: Graduates will demonstrate an ability to design Engineering and Management systems, components and processes as per user defined needs and specifications.

    • Modern tool usage: Graduates will demonstrate the skills to use the latest tools and techniques in Mechanical Engineering, software and equipment to analyze and solve problems.

    • The engineer and society: Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to the mechanical engineering

    • Environment and sustainability: Graduates will be aware of the impact of engineering processes on Environment, Society and of contemporary issues and shall be able to provide suitable solutions for the same.

    • Ethics: Graduates will reveal an understanding of their professional as well as ethical and social responsibilities.

    • Individual and team work: Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams, and in multidisciplinary settings.

    • Communication: Communicate effectively on complex engineering activities with the engineering community and with society at large, such as, being able to comprehend and write effective reports and design documentation, make effective presentations, and give and receive clear instructions.

    • Project management and finance: Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the engineering and management principles and apply these to one’s own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects and in multidisciplinary environments.

    • Life-long learning: Recognize the need for, and have the preparation and ability to engage inindependent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change.

      Graduating student shall be able to: 

    • Identify, comprehend and find solutions to Mechanical Engineering problems in real life situations.

    • Apply the principles of manufacturing engineering and technology to develop techno commercial skills.

    • Apply fundamentals to design and analyze the thermo-hydraulic systems.

    • Design and analyze mechanical components and processes to predict the behavior of engineering systems. 

    • Perform effectively first level managerial responsibilities of large/medium engineering organizations. 

    Dear Students,
    Welcome to the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Marathwada Institute of Technology Aurangabad. It gives me immense pleasure to lead the Department of Mechanical Engineering. The main objective of the department is to provide a quality engineering education through sound theoretical knowledge, hands on laboratory as well as computational skill and exposure to recent technologies by visiting industries. The process of learning is extremely important in life.
    How to learn, what to learn, why to learn and where to learn play a vital role in developing the capabilities in students while studying in Institutes as well as performing as engineers in their career and living as part of society.
    The students of the Department actively involved in various competitions of National and International repute like ROBOCON, SAE-BAJA, SAE-SUPRA and technical competitions in various institutions and also credited number of Winner titles to the Department. The Department has various student’s chapters like ASME, SAE and ISTE. The Department also conducts the guiding sessions and provides facility for competitive exams like, GATE, GRE, TOEFL and Engineering Services. Organizing number of short term training programs, workshop and seminars from time to time for faculties for sharing and updating the technical knowledge is a regular feature of the Department.
    An interactive relationship is maintained between the students and staff which enable the students to develop a sound foundation in the stream in conducive environment. This is also reflected in our campus and off-campus placement. Our students also proceed to pursue their higher education in top Universities in globe like University of California Berkeley, Wayne State University, University of Wisconsin, etc. Students also have secured admissions for higher education in top most institutes like IITs, IIMs and NITs in India.
    The Department faculties along with students are also involved in various Research activities. The major Research Areas are: Design and Vibration, CAD/CAM/CAE and Manufacturing, Machining and Micro Machining, Composite Materials, Material Characterization, Nano Materials and Bio Materials, Cryogenic, Energy and Sustainability, Internal Combustion Engines, Alternative Fuels, Computational Fluid Dynamics, Robotics and Mechatronics, Service System Design, Reliability
    Department of Mechanical Engineering looks forward to contribute in solving the technical challenges of the society with active participation.

    Thank you for visiting us.
