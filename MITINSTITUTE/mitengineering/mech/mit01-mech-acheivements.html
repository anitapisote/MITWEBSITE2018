Tata Technologies Ltd (TTL)

Tata Technologies Ltd (TTL) initiated " Ready Engineer" program for Mechanical Engineering Students. In this program TATA Technologies shall impart quality training in the "Design Engineering" domain to Second Year and Third Year Mechanical Engineering students. The main intent behind this initiative is to better equip young engineers to increase awareness and industry connect. Today on 23 February 2018, Marathwada Institute of Technology Aurangabad signed MoU with TATA Technologies Ltd to run Ready Engineer Program at the Institute for Mechanical Engineering Students. Mr. Anubhav Kapoor (Head-Corporate Sustainability, General Counsel & Company Secretary, TTL), Dr Nilesh G Patil (Princiapal, MIT E), Dr Subhash Lahane (Head, MED), Dr D V Nehete (VP), Prof S B Pankade (TPO) were present for MoU signing program.

iCMMD-2017

Marathwada Institute of Technology (MIT), Aurangabad (M.S.), India has organized 2nd International Conference on Materials, Manufacturing and Design (iCMMD-2017) in collaboration with Dr.Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University (BATU), Lonere (M.S.), India. The conference was held on 11th and 12th December, 2017.
FEA 2016

On date 11th November 2016, one day workshop on FEA Technology was conducted at MIT Aurangabad. Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) in conjunction with MIT Aurangabad conducted this workshop. This workshop was useful for the engineering college teachers and person from the industries in Mechanical and civil branch. Around 55 no people participated in this workshop.
