Infrastructure
Lab No:
Hydraulic Machine

Understand and be familiar with the concepts of energy flow, including losses, in turbo machines.Understand the different types of hydraulic turbines, their ranges of basic parameters and characteristics of hydraulic turbines.

Lab No: 212
Refrigeration & Air Conditioning

The objective of this lab is to study and demonstrate the refrigeration and air-conditioning cycles, processes as well as the controls, components and accessories associated with the system.

Lab No: 215
Metrology and Mechanical Measurement

To learn the basic principles of linear & angular measurements and surface topology.To study different methods of measurement of temperature, pressure.To do the statistical analysis of data.

Lab No:
Internal combustion Engine

To make students familiar with operating characteristics of internal combustion engines.Students are expected to understand & analyze the fundamentals and working of Internal Combustion Engines to meet the modern requirements.

Lab No: 216
CAD

To provide a holistic approach in learning through well designed courses involving fundamental concepts and state-of-the-art techniques in the field of CAD – CAM.To be able to design, analyse and manufacture real life systems and components.

Lab No: G4
Engineering Metallurgy

To impart a fundamental knowledge about extraction of Steel & Cast Iron, their heat treatment process & industrial use.To impart sound knowledge of different materials with their selection, properties for industrial application.

Lab No:
Automobile Engineering

To impart the knowledge of different devises, methods, and accessories of automobiles. To provide students a good environment to understand the working of automobile/ vehicle components.

Lab No: 210
Thermal Engineering

To illustrate the physical concepts of Thermodynamics developed in the class using working models.To provide knowledge on testing of properties of fuels ,lubricating oils and steam.

Lab No: 217
Heat Transfer

To impart practical knowledge on Design, analysis and amount of heat exchange in various modes of heat transfer by studying various thermal systems like heat exchangers, pin fin, including condensation and boiling for several geometries.

Lab No: 213
Automatic Control system

Analysing a control system with the help of Block Diagram Analysis.Explaining the working of different components of control systems.Differentiating the use of different controllers in control systems.

Lab No: 214
Dynamics of Machine

To impart practical knowledge on design, analysis of motion and the dynamical forces acting on mechanical systems composed of links; gears; governors; gyroscopes; and cams, Also role of friction in belt; rope; chain ; brakes; dynamometers, Also understand analysis of vibration and balancing of machine.
