It goes without saying that no knowledge is useful and innovative unless it is rooted in fundamentals. For engineers of the emerging world, in which competition has become global and all pervasive, a sound knowledge of the basics of Physics, Chemistry and a mastery over highly advanced mathematical techniques serve as fundamentals along with strong English communication skills. The Department of Basic Sciences & Humanities plays a pivotal role for implementation of engineering and technology programs conducted at Marathwada Institute of Technology. The Department always strives to strengthen the basic knowledge of subjects like Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and English necessary for solving real life problems of the higher classes and society. The Department has experienced, knowledgeable and qualified faculty which believes in life long learning. The departments has ICT enabled class rooms and well equipped laboratories with facilities and are established to provide adequate opportunities for students to learn practical aspects of respective subjects. Further to develop all-round personality of First Year Engineering & Technology students the department hosts co-curricular and extra-curricular activities e.g. expert talks, seminars, visits and “Techno-Blitz (a event only for first year students of all affiliated colleges)” on regular basis. The Department has initiated a platform for students “SAFE” a Students Association for First Year Engineering from this academic year.
