Infrastructure
Lab No: 301
Electronic Devices and Circuits Lab

This Lab covers practical aspects of Basic Electronics Engineering. It is equipped with CRO, Function Generator, Dual Power Supply, DSO, etc along with various kits.

Lab No: 302
Linear Integrated Circuits and Network Lab

In this lab, practicals based on IC 741 analog IC and network analysis are covered. It consists of equipments like CRO, Function Generator, Dual Power Supply, DSO, etc and various LIC and Network kits.

Lab No: 303
Instrumentation and Control Lab

This lab covers practical related to process control and automation along-with electronic measurement and instrumentation. This lab has PID Controller, Syncro, Type 0, 1, and 2 system and Water level Measurement kits. It also has PLC setup for performing experiments related to industrial Automation Operations.

Lab No: 304
Digital Switching and Microprocessor Lab

In this lab practicals related to subjects like Digital Logic Design, Microprocessor and Microcontroller, and Embedded System. This lab is equipped with various Processor and controller kits alongwith various interfacing boards. It has softwares like EMU 8086, Kiel, Flash Magic, SCARM, etc.

Lab No: 305 A
Communication Lab

In this lab various practicals related to Communication engineering subjects like Microwave Engineering, Satellite communication, etc. This lab includes RADAR Trainer, Satellite Trainer, Logic Analyser, Microwave Test Bench and various other kits.

Lab No: 305 B
Research Embedded Lab

• To enable students to program, simulate and test the 8051, PIC, AVR, ARM and MSP 430 based circuits and their interface. • To enable students to program various devices using Kiel, Flash Magic, EMU 8086, etc.
• To provide platform for the students to do multidisciplinary projects.

Lab No: 306 A
Digital Signal Processing Lab

This lab covers practical related to Digital Signal Processing and Image Processing. This lab has 20 PC’s equipped with MATLAB software.

Lab No: 306 B
Departmental Library

The department library consists of 1003 books related to subjects of Engineering. This library facilitates students to issue and use books/magazines other than the central library facilty.

Lab No: 407
Printed Circuit Board Lab

This lab covers practicals like Electronic System Design and Electronic Workshop. It is equipped with Dark Room, Yellow Room, Etching Machine, Drilling Machine, Photo Resist, Photo Film, Tinning Machine, Double sided pin machine, etc.

Lab No: 313 A
Virtual Laboratory

This lab covers practicals like Robotics, Virtual lab. This lab consists of 24 PC’s. It is allotted as Nodal Centre by IIT, Bombay

Lab No: 313 B
Project and Computer Lab

This lab serves as project lab for students. It consists of 20 PC’s.

Lab No: 317
Power Electronics Lab

This lab covers practicals related to Power Electronics. It is equipped with High voltage supply kit and small kits for testing characteristics for components like SCR, DIAC, TRIAC, IGBT, etc.

Lab No: 318-B
VLSI and Circuit Lab

This lab is equipped with SPARTAN 2 and SPARTAN 6 kits and Zed Boards. There are also circuit simulation softwares like Xilinx ISE 9.1 for programming in VHDL.

Hall No: 316
Seminar Hall

The digital class room is having Soft board, Projector, Sound system. This classroom is having capacity of 80 students

Class Room No: 408 to 411
Classroom

The classrooms are well aerated and illuminated with pleasant light. They have seating capacity of 80 students.
