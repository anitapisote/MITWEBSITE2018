  • Kamalnayan Bajaj Hospital, Aurangabad. (27th March 2018)

    Purpose of Visit was to Study Installation of Air Conditioning and Fire Fighting System in a Building Like Hospital. Students studied the All Water system of AC in the central plant room, AHU and AC ducting of the ICU/OT and New Medical Store for which the work was undergoing, Grills and diffusers installed at the recently completed areas and cooling tower installed at the site. They also studied the firefighting system in the building, various installations like Automatic sprinklers, Fire detectors, hose reel, portable extinguishers and fire hydrants installed at site and its pump room. It was an informative, interesting and a successful visit. As architecture students we learnt the design considerations for various building services.

    • 27 students and one Faculty member.
    • Site visited :Kamalnayan Bajaj Hospital, Aurangabad.

    Siddharth Swimming Pool ,Aurangabad. (21stMarch 2018)

    The main objective of the visit was to observe the actual constructional details and working of swimming pool. Students were already introduced with the working and construction details of swimming pool. There were two swimming pools: Olympic Size Pool and Learners Pool. Students saw the necessary requirements associated with Pool like Changing rooms, Aeration tank etc. detailed filtration cycle was explained to the students. Siddharth Swimming Pool is having all the facilities like viewers gallery, diving boards, jumping steps, balancing tank. Students learnt and understand the detail working of the swimming pool. They have been guided by the safety and security measures taken while designing the pools. It was an explanatory, inspiring and a successful visit. As architecture students we learnt the working and construction of Swimming Pools.

    • 22 students and 02 faculty members
    • Site visited : Siddharth Swimming Pool,Aurangabad.

    SALIM ALI GARDEN (12 March 2018).

    Purpose of site visit is to study physical, visual & climatological aspects of site for the project Weekend Home. Physical aspect includes site measurements, topography, vegetation and existing features of the site. Visual aspect includes site surrounding, study of impact of surrounding on site . And climatological aspect includes study of wind pattern, sun path for the site . Also students were made to study the site thoroughly and do the sketches in the groups. It was an informative, interesting and a successful visit. As architecture students they learnt the existing site conditions for design project.

    • 38 students and 02 faculty members

    TK VENTURES SHOPPING COMPLEX : (9thMARCH, 2018)

    The site visit was arranged for shopping complex at Akashwani, Aurangabad. We left the college campus at 9:15am and were back by 11:15am. The site study for the project began with introducing students to the RCC structure and RMC plant. The chief engineer of TK VENTURES introduced the RCC structure and how it is erected/casted on the site with all construction details and working of the structure.

  • Residential construction :Jai Nilaya Residency, Shahnoorwadi Aurangabad.(10th Aug 2017)

    Objectives of visit ,to study the construction details of RCC staircase.Students got to see the practical implementation to the theoretical knowledge and RCC reinforcement being laid out on construction site. It was easy for students to understand RCC staircase while observing the construction process live. It was a great experience as we actually studied there how reinforcement of staircase is laid and how concreting is done. There we got to know about the types of the steel bars which are laid in staircase i.e. main bars, stirrups, chair bars and how much covering we should provide in R.C.C staircase. Steps taken for curing and shuttering of RCC staircase.

    • 16 students and one Faculty member.
    • Site visited : Jai Nilaya Residency, Shahnoorwadi Aurangabad.

    Photos

    Residential construction Amrut Sai Plaza, Aurangabad. (13thSept. 2017)

    Purpose of Visit was to Study basics of building construction and different structural components of building in a Residential building. More understanding about construction technology of buildings and different methods of constructing, plus explore and identify different types of building materials. Students studied the Foundation of a building, how the Reinforcement and Forming of Columns, Beams and Foundation of a building is done. They also studied the bending of Bars, mixing of Concrete. Students also studied different structural components of a building and there construction. It was an informative, interesting and a successful visit. As first year architecture students learnt the basic building materials, components and construction methods for residential buildings.

    • 36 students and one Faculty member.
    • Site visited : Amrut Sai Plaza, Aurangabad.

    STUDY TOUR TO RAJASTHAN (9TH TO 18TH AUGUST 2017)

    A 10 day study tour was organized by the department of architecture, MIT to Rajasthan for the students of S.Y., T.Y., and FOURTH YEAR from 9th to 18th August 2017. Total 33 students and 3 teachers had gone for the tour.A study tour part of the curriculum for the students of architecture enables them to visit places of great historic, cultural and ethnic importance. This brings to light the various features that are important to architectural designing, such as the history of its origin and evolution; the various styles and influence of the political and economic situation of the era; concepts of town planning; etc. A practical approach to studying the theoretical concepts makes learning an easy and enjoyable process.

    • 33 students and 3 faculty members
    • Site visited :STUDY TOUR TO RAJASTHAN

  • STUDY TRIP TO “KEKI MOOS ART GALLERY”(March 27th, 2015)
