About Us

Department of Civil Engineering was established in 2004 and offers Bachelor of Technology (B. Tech.) in Civil Engineering. The department is devoted to research and development and is concerned with solving the engineering problems of industry and society.

To develop the department as Centre of Excellence in Civil Engineering by using modern teaching learning techniques with global perspectives to make technologically superior and ethically strong engineers.

To impart knowledge to civil engineering students for the betterment of the society by

  • Educating and training in the latest technologies
  • Developing self-confidence in tackling diverse problems of civil engineering
  • Imbibing human values

  • To prepare the students with strong fundamental knowledge in Mathematics, Science, English and Engineering Sciences so as to enable them to analyze the Civil Engineering related problems (Preparation).

  • To develop ability among the students for acquiring technical knowledge in the subjects and gaining the technical skills. (Core competence and professionalism).

  • To provide opportunities for students to work with all related fields of engineering enlarge the ability among the students to understand the different industrial environments (Breadth).

  • To promote the students for continuous learning, research and development with strong professional moral and ethical values. (Learning environment)

  • An ability to apply knowledge of mathematics, science, and engineering to solve Civil engineering problems.

  • An ability to identify, formulate, design and conduct experiments, as well as to analyze and interpret data.

  • An ability to design a system, component, or process to meet desired needs within realistic constraints such as economic, environmental, social, safety, and sustainability.

  • Use research based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data and synthesizes the information to provide valid conclusion.

  • An ability to use the techniques, skills, and modern engineering tools necessary for civil engineering practice.

  • Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to access societal, health, legal, and cultural issues and consequent responsibilities relevant to professional engineering practices.

  • Evaluate the impact of professional engineering solutions in society and environment context and demonstrate the knowledge of sustainable infrastructural development.

  • An understanding of professional and ethical responsibility to extend the social benefit of the civil engineering project.

  • Function effectively as individual and team member or leader in diverse situation and multidisciplinary settings.

  • An ability to communicate effectively to handle complex engineering activities with the engineering community and the society at large, and should posses the skill of technical writing and effective presentation.

  • Demonstrate the knowledge of engineering and management principles and apply these in one’s work to manage multidisciplinary projects.

  • Recognition of the need for, and an ability to engage in independent life-long learning to incorporate technological innovations.

  • An ability to carry out quality analysis and assurance check.

  • An ability to conduct quantity survey and frame the specifications.

  • An ability to estimate to possible disasters and suggest appropriate remedial technique.

You are welcome to Department of Civil Engineering. The main objective of department is to prepare students for successful career in Industry, Research and Academics to meet the needs of growing technology.

All the labs of the department are well equipped. In addition to the regular academic activity, the department also offers testing and consultancy to various government and private agencies by involving the students, which helps students to get actual practical knowledge. Many of the students are involved in large number of social responsibility and extension activities under National Service Scheme (NSS) and Unnat Maharashtra Abhiyan (UMA). The students are always encouraged to excel in co-curricular as well as extracurricular activities.

We have highly qualified and experienced faculties with diverse area of expertise. I am confident that your education at our department will bring you up as a technologically superior and ethically strong engineer.

Thank you for visiting us.

Dr. A. G. Dahake
