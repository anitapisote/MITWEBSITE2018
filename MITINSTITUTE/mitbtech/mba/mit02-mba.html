To be a leading department of management science for developing ethical managerial leaders and entrepreneurs.

We are committed to provide student centric approach in management education to cultivate the skills of the students in becoming professionally competent in business & industry and committed towards societal development.

  • Graduates will have knowledge and ability to perform as management professionals in business and industry.

  • Graduates will have attitude towards continued learning throughout their career.

  • Graduates will have the positive perspectives and skills that create productive managerial leaders.

  • Apply knowledge of management theories and practices to solve business problems.

  • Foster Analytical and critical thinking abilities for data-based decision making.

  • Ability to develop Value based Leadership ability.

  • Ability to understand, analyze and communicate global, economic, legal, and ethical aspects of business.

  • Ability to lead themselves and others in the achievement of organizational goals, contributing effectively to a team environment.

Dear Students,
It gives me great pleasure to welcome you to our MBA Program, which will give you a sight of Management Sciences at MIT. At MIT, we offer a program in wholesome management education namely MBA (Master of Business Administration). MBA is a university affiliated program with its structure, syllabus and content as defined by the University, approved by AICTE and well recognized all over.
At MIT we go that extra mile, over and above University stipulated requirements for MBA, in delivering what it takes to be industry ready. Building up of competencies and uniqueness of personality are underlying factors of our management education. Case based methodology, industry interface; personality & attitude development form a major part of our teaching plan. In fact MIT is one such institute whose pioneering efforts in offering a quality focused management program has made it one of its kind in this part of Maharashtra.
Our dedicated faculty, industry interactions, involvement of academically inclined guest faculty, state of the art library, computer savvy methods, conducive infrastructure and above all our approach to do whatever it takes to shape future managers has enabled us to stand out from the crowd.
