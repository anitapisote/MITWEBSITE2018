About Us

Agricultural Engineering is basically an area of engineering concerned with the applications of knowledge and principles of all the engineering branches in the field of agriculture.

To achieve excellence in agricultural engineering education, by applying knowledge of agricultural and allied branches of engineering to develop professionally competent and socially committed engineers.

To impart quality education and training in the field of agricultural engineering by providing conducive environment for teaching-learning and innovation to face emerging technological and social challenges.

  • Graduates will demonstrate knowledge of innovative technologies needed for the practice of agricultural engineering

  • Graduates will develop skills necessary to design the process to evaluate and implement problem solutions of agriculture

  • Graduates will develop continuous professional and technical growth so as to be licensed as a professional engineer and successful leader

  • Graduates will be capable of addressing issues of ethical and social impact of engineering practice

  • Use his/her expertise in planning the judicious utilization of natural recourses and their management through advanced soil and water conservation techniques and various irrigation methods with the skill of data interpretation.

  • Design and develop of farm implement and stead with economically feasible, agro and energy technologies for sustainable agriculture

  • Apply the comprehensive knowledge of engineering properties of agricultural produce for upgrading the unit operation resulting in effective value addition and quality control

  • Apply knowledge of mathematics, science and engineering

  • Identify, formulate and analyze engineering problems

  • Design the system components or processes to meet desired needs such as economic, environmental, social, political, ethical, health and safety.

  • Use research based knowledge and research methods to provide valid conclusions

  • Use the techniques and modern engineering tools necessary for engineering practices

  • Apply contextual knowledge to access societal health and safety issues

  • Understand impact of agricultural engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts

  • Apply professional ethics and responsibilities

  • Function effectively as an individual and as a leader

  • Communicate effectively on agricultural engineering activities with engineering community and society at large

  • Understand and apply engineering and management principles to manage projects

  • Engage in lifelong learning process with the context of technological change

Dear Students,
On behalf of Department of Agricultural Engineering, I welcome you on our portal to get know-how of our department.Agricultural Engineering Department is striving hard towards goal to achieve excellence in agricultural engineering education in order to develop professionally competent and socially committed engineers to mitigate the continuously rising needs of agriculture and food fraternity.

Agricultural Engineering Department of MIT is one of its kinds in Maharashtra State offering B. Tech. in Agricultural Engineering and M. Tech. in Food Processing Technology as AICTE and DTE approved courses. We have highly qualified, experienced and specialized faculty, spacious laboratories with high end modern instruments equipped for bringing excellence in research and innovation.

We undertake rigorous and intensive integrated teaching methods, interaction with experts, industry and field visits and make students constantly aware about the present trends and future challenges in the field of agriculture and food processing. We are sure that our engineers will emerge as assets not only to this department and organization they belong but also to the country and society, at large.

I am confident that our efforts directed towards imparting quality education and training in the field of agricultural engineering and food processing will help to create ready to use engineers for tomorrow’s needs to take up emerging technological and social challenges. I applaud the ingenious and earnest efforts undertaken by my colleagues and students for their accomplishment and wish all them very success for future endeavors.

Thank you for visiting us.

Dr. Hanuman Pandurangrao Bobade
