ESCO Approved By BEE


MIT is an Approved ESCO = Empanelled as Energy Service Company approved by Bureau of Energy Efficiency(BEE), Ministry of Power, Go. MIT conducts energy audit training for industry and run MEDA supported short term courses.

MIT works with various state energy development authorities like MEDA, GEDA, CEDA, MPEDA, etc which relates to conducting various programs of energy audit, awareness program for energy conservation and involve student’s participation and training. MIT ESCO is an active member of Energy Cell of Chamber of Marathwada Industries and Agriculture (CMIA).
